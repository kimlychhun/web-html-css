About Our Shop

CK Phone Shop , established in 2000, has quickly become the Cambodia's leading mobile phone retailer at the most visible location of Phnom Penh. We endeavor to continue to provide unlimited access to our customers to the information of brand-new and used mobile handsets and accessories. Our valued customers can always gain extensive access to collective information which is useful and up-to-date about mobile phones of all popular brands.
